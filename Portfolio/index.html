Video Game Sales Data Analysis Using Excel

This project was created using Excel exclusively. I utilized a video game sales dataset that I found on Kaggle.com. After performing an exploratory data analysis, I arrived at a few conclusions. Firstly, sales in North America were consistently higher than in Europe and Japan between 1980 and 2016. Secondly, the Playstation 2 platform has sold the most in terms of total sales. Thirdly, Nintendo is the leading publisher from 1980 until 2016. Lastly, I found that video games belonging to the action genre comprise the largest proportion of global sales. The techniques employed for this analysis include:

  • Data Cleaning
  • Pivot Tables
  • Formulas
  • xLookup
  • Charts
  • Excel Dashboard
  • Slicers
